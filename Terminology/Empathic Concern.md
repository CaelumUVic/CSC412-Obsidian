
#terminology
## Definition
"The emotional response of sharing and caring about another person's emotional state, often leading to a motivation to help them."

Is closely related to [[Cognitive Empathy]] but involves feeling for others, not just understanding them

## Link to Empathy
Empathic concern represents the emotional response of feeling for others, beyond merely understanding their perspective through [[Cognitive Empathy]]. This component involves sharing in others' emotional states and developing a motivation to help them. By fostering genuine care for others' wellbeing, empathic concern drives positive behaviors and strengthens social connections.
